Do we actually jump out of planes?
Yes we do! We do all of our jumps through Skydive Fargo!
Do I need prior experience to jump?
Almost all of our members are first time jumpers. And most first timers will do tandem jumps(instructor jumps with you). All that is required is a short safety briefing.
How often are jumps?
We try to plan several days per semester where students can all go together. However you are welcome to signup whenever.
Where do we jump?
Jumps are either done at the West Fargo Municial Airport(10min from NDSU) or the Moorhead Airport(20mins from NDSU).
How do I book?
To book your jump go to skydivefargo.com and press book my skydive.
How do I join?
The official way to join is joining our myNDSU page. This will get you access to all of our emails and communications. Follow our Instagram and Facebook to stay up to date as well.
